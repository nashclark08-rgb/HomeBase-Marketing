How it works

Set up in five minutes. Use it every day.

01

Create your family

Sign up with email or Google, name your family, and you'll get a unique family code to share with your partner, co-parent, or other adults helping with the kids.

02

Add your children

Each child gets their own profile — name, year, school, photo, and a colour. Their packing list, events, and assessments all live there.

03

Add events and routines

Soccer Tuesdays. Piano Thursdays. School excursions. Birthday parties. Set drop-off and pick-up so everyone knows who's responsible.

04

Stay in sync, automatically

Everyone in the family sees the same schedule, packing lists, and dinner plan in real time. Snap a photo of a school invite and HomeBase auto-fills the details.

Privacy & Your Data

Last updated: April 2026

HomeBase is built so your family's information stays private to your family. Plain-English explanation of what we store and how.

What we store

  • Your name and email (for sign in)
  • Family name and a unique family code
  • Children's names, ages, year level, school, optional photo
  • Other adults' names, contact info, and photos when you add them
  • Events, packing lists, dinners, shopping lists, assessments, gifts, play dates
  • Your weather location and notification preferences

Where it's stored

All data lives in Google Firebase — the same secure platform used by thousands of apps. Your data is stored in Sydney, Australia for fast access and Australian data residency.

Who can see your data

Only people in your family who have signed in with your family code. We use Firebase's authentication rules to enforce this technically — no one else, including search engines or other HomeBase users, can see your information.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't share it with advertisers
  • We don't track you across other websites
  • We don't read your emails or messages
  • We don't use your data to train AI

OCR (smart-scan) processing

When you scan an assessment sheet or birthday invite, the photo is processed entirely on your device using a free open-source library (Tesseract.js). The image never leaves your phone or browser. Only the extracted text is saved.

Deleting your data

  • Remove a child or adult: tap them in Family → Edit → Delete
  • Leave the family: Settings → Danger Zone → Leave This Family
  • Delete the entire family permanently: Settings → Danger Zone (admin only). Cannot be undone.

Children's privacy

HomeBase is designed for parents to manage information about their children. Children's data should only be added by their parent or legal guardian. We don't allow children under 13 to create their own accounts.

Your rights

You can request a copy of all your data, correct anything wrong, or have it deleted at any time. Email nashclark08@gmail.com. Under Australian Privacy Principles and the GDPR (where applicable), you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information.
